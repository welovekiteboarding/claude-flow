About Toronto Wakesurf

We are Toronto's premier wakesurfing school, dedicated to providing safe, professional, and fun wakesurfing experiences on Lake Ontario's pristine waters.

With over 10 years of experience teaching water sports, our certified instructors have helped thousands of students discover the joy of wakesurfing. We pride ourselves on our safety record, professional equipment, and personalized instruction approach.
